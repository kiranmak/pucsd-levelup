Sample hackathon assignments

1. Write a program that takes a word as input and prints all possible permutations of legal words that exist for a given dictionary. 

2. Imagine you are bidding for cricketers in an IPL auction for your team. You have a given budget and your goal is to form a team based on your criteria. Write a program that will select the players for your team that meet your criteria at an optimal cost and within your budget. 

Input:
a. Pool of players available along with their attributes and cost
b. No. of players that will be put for auction (may be less than the total pool size)
c. Sequence of players that are put up auction
d. Your team budget
e. Your criteria in terms of player attributes

Output:
a. Names of players selected in your team
b. Total expense
